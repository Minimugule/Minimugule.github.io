Athletics in Brittany

Summary: This Rshiny project focuses on studying athletics clubs in Brittany. Each year in athletics, inter-club competitions are held, covering all disciplines of this sport, such as running, throwing, jumping, walking, etc. The objective is to rank clubs at the national level. For each category, two men and two women participate, and points are awarded to the club based on their performances. The goal is to accumulate as many points as possible. However, clubs do not have computer tools to calculate these points, forcing them to consult tables to determine the points awarded for their performances. We were asked to develop an application capable of performing this calculation and thus assigning the corresponding points to each event.

NBA Salary Prediction

Summary: This R project aims to predict NBA players' salaries. The intersection between NBA players' performance and their remuneration has always been a captivating subject. Our project delves into this dynamic, examining data from 1996 to 2017 to understand how individual performances influence players' contracts and salaries. Through an Econometric approach, we explore the links between on-court performances and players' financial compensation. In this context, we ask the following question: To what extent do individual basketball players' performances influence their salary?

This analysis is part of the broader context of current debates on professional athletes' salaries, offering significant insights for basketball enthusiasts, economic analysts, and actors in the sports world.
